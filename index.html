About the Initiative

Under the patronage of Sheikha Shamma bint Sultan bin Khalifa Al Nahyan

Our Mission

The Shamma Initiative is an environmental community platform that aims to spread the culture of tree planting and recycling among community members, especially targeting youth and children, within the framework of UAE Vision 2030 for environmental sustainability.

Vision 2030

Aligned with the UAE's commitment to environmental sustainability, our initiative contributes to the national vision of creating a greener, more sustainable future for generations to come.

Our Goals

Building a sustainable future for the UAE

Raise Awareness

Increase awareness about the importance of tree planting as a means to combat desertification and improve air quality

Build Strong Institution

Create a strong institutional image for the project under Sheikha Shamma's name

Encourage Participation

Promote community participation from individuals and institutions in tree planting and recycling

Lead in Tree Planting

Become the leading charitable organization specialized in tree planting

Promote Recycling

Contribute to raising awareness about recycling and its importance to the environment

Environmental Impact

Create measurable positive environmental impact across the UAE
