About the Initiative

Under the patronage of Sheikha Shamma Bint Mohammed Bin Khalid Al Nahyan

Our Mission

The Shamma Initiative is an environmental community platform that aims to spread the culture of tree planting and recycling among community members, especially targeting youth and children, within the framework of UAE Vision 2030 for environmental sustainability.

Vision 2030

Aligned with the UAE's commitment to environmental sustainability, our initiative contributes to the national vision of creating a greener, more sustainable future for generations to come.

Our Programs

Year-round activities and initiatives

Distribution of Recycling Machines

Spreading recycling culture

  • Goal: The objective is to spread the culture of recycling among community members.
  • They provide users with instant rewards (such as mobile credit or points) for used water bottles and beverage cans.
  • Quantitative Goal: To provide 2,000 machines by 2030 to become the main channel for collecting solid waste for recycling.

"Environmental Education Bag" Program (Awareness Kits)

Enhancing environmental awareness in schools

  • The kit is distributed to students in schools to enhance environmental awareness and encourage correct environmental practices.
  • Its contents include awareness stories, interactive activities, and recycling tools.

Mobile Library for Environmental Education

Spreading knowledge across the Emirates

  • It is a mobile educational library aimed at spreading environmental knowledge and educating the community in various regions.
  • It conducts regular tours throughout the Emirates to achieve comprehensive access for all community members.
